Welcome to the Tutorial

Your guide to understanding on how to transfer a fresh leaf into herbarium specimens

The Tutorial

Collecting

Keep an eye out for plants that are close to blooming, and collect a sample as soon as the flowers open! Always collect healthy, pest-free, and dry specimens.

Describing

When collecting, attach a label to the specimen recording: name of plant, date of collection, collector, site of collection and original source of plant. Remember to record the colour using the RHS Colour Chart.

Pressing

Use hardboard or plywood to make a press. Sandwich your specimen in the inner layer, blotting paper, then sheet of foam, then corrugated card then finally place it inside the press. Think of it like a burger.

Mounting

After the pressing process, stick the specimens onto an acid-free A4 paper using neutral-pH glue and gummed linen hanging tape.

Storage

Keep your specimens in somewhere safe, like a book or a plastic bag then freeze for 72 hours to kill any pests.
